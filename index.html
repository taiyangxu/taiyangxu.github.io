Here are my Preprints/Publications.

I'm an analyst who place the particular interests in the area of mathematical physics and applied mathematics. More specifically, I am interested in the Integrable Systems (IS), Random Matrix Theory (RMT) as well as related topics. Here, I exhibit some key words of my research interest below.

Integrable PDEs, Random matrices theory, Determinantal point processes, Orthogonal polynomials, Asymptotic analysis, Riemann-Hilbert (RH) problems, Special functions, Painlevé equations ......

The application of asymptotic methods, special functions, PDE analysis as well as complex analytical technique is the central to the work. Riemann-Hilbert (RH) problem is the main tool used by me to study physically meaningful quantities arising in the fields of integrable differential equations and random matrices theory, in particular their asymptotics associated to special functions and Painlevé family equations.
